I tend to show up when something should be working, but isn't. The strategy looks right. The team is strong. There's momentum on paper. But underneath, something's off — and no one can quite name it. I work across product strategy and monetization, mostly in the creator and media ecosystem, where there's still a wide gap between attention and actual value. The throughline is simple: take something ambiguous but promising, and turn it into a system that generates value.
