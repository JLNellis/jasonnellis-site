04 Formats

How I show up.

Keynote A single sustained argument — built around a strong thesis, not a survey of the landscape. Best for audiences who want a perspective, not a recap. 45–60 min
Panel I hold a position in a panel. I'm not interested in being agreeable for the sake of it — the value is in the friction and the genuine disagreements between people who've built things. 30–45 min
Fireside A conversation rather than a presentation. Works best when the interviewer is willing to go somewhere unscripted. I prefer this format when the audience is relatively small. 20–40 min
Podcast / Interview Building Value is my own show, but I'm open to guest appearances on podcasts that take strategy and media seriously — not just creator-economy hype cycles. Remote · In-person
Workshop Hands-on sessions for leadership teams working through a specific strategic question — go-to-market, product direction, audience monetization. Limited availability. Half or full day
