Thinking About

What's holding my attention.

The gap between what AI companies say about their products and what operators actually experience building with them. There's a lot of distance there right now, and it's getting wider before it gets narrower.

Also: what it means to build an audience in a moment when distribution channels are consolidating and differentiating simultaneously. The creator economy premise — that individual voice has durable value — is being tested in interesting ways.
